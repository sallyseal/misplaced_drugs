About Website

Website Name was created by the Department of Bioinformatics at Imperial College London. It contains 332 unique multi-target drug entries each with their respective targets. Each drug entry contains detailed information including pharmacology and targete interactions. Structural information from PDB is available for each drug target and when possible the drug-target bound conformation is highlighted.
